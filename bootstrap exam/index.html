FAQ

Below are answers to some of the frequently asked questions we receive about Slate Ed. and Slate courses.

Can I start for free and pay later?
Yes, you absolutely can start your course in the audit track and then upgrade to the verified track later, so long as the upgrade deadline has not passed.
Are courses accessible to learners with disabilities?
EdX strives to create an innovative online-learning platform that promotes accessibility for all learners, regardless of any physical limitation that they might have.
What is my dashboard?
Your dashboard gives you access to the courses and programs that you have enrolled in, and to information such as course start and end dates, certificates, and email settings.
How can I pay for a course?
Udemy supports several different payment methods, depending on your account country and location.
What if I don’t like a course I purchased?
We want you to be satisfied, so all eligible courses purchased on Slate can be refunded within 30 days.
What do Slate courses include?
The foundation of each course are its lectures, which can include videos, slides, and text. In addition, instructors can add resources and various types of practice activities
Where can I go for help?
You can ask the instructor or email us at slate.support@gmail.com.
Do I have to pay any fees in order to become an instructor?
There is no fee to be an instructor on Slate. Instructors are paid according to a revenue share model.
Are there any requirements that my course must meet?
Yes. Courses must have at least 30 minutes of video content and at least 5 lectures or learning modules.
